What do we do?

We improve your workout!

At GymCore, we believe that proper form is key to achieving your fitness goals and avoiding injuries. That's why we've developed an app that utilizes artificial intelligence to analyze your workout form in real time and provide you with personalized feedback to help you perform each exercise correctly. Our app is designed to be your virtual personal trainer, always by your side to ensure that you're executing each exercise with proper form. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned gym-goer, our AI-powered technology provides you with customized cues to help you improve your form and technique, reducing your risk of injury and maximizing your results. With our app, you no longer have to worry about whether you're doing an exercise correctly or risking harm to your body. Our intelligent feedback system helps you identify and correct mistakes as they happen, ensuring that you're getting the most out of your workouts and improving your form with every repetition.
